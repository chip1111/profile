Tiramisu Facts

Meaning

‘Tiramisu’ is an Italian word that can be translated literally as "pick me up". Although a more poetic translation of it is simply: "take me to heaven". More accurate.

Origins

Look at this guy! What a legend. That's chef Roberto Linguanotto (aka the greatest man in the world) who created the dessert at Le Beccherie restaurant in the city of Treviso in the early 1960s.

A Big Tiramisu

On 25 May 2015 the world record for largest Tiramisu was set in the small Italian city of Gemona del Friuli. Its weight was 3015kg. One guy ate the whole thing. Apparently.
